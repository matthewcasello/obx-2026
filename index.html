🗺️ Around South Nags Head
🐟
Fish Heads Bar & Grill ~2 min · walkable
Right on the Outer Banks Fishing Pier, two blocks up the beach road. Casual eats, drinks over the water, live music — the closest "we don't feel like cooking" option.
🗼
Bodie Island Lighthouse ~10 min south
Classic black-and-white striped lighthouse with a boardwalk over the marsh. Climbing tickets in season; great for a low-key morning.
🚤
Oregon Inlet Fishing Center ~10 min south
Charter fishing fleet HQ. Even without booking a boat, watching the catch come in around 4pm is free entertainment.
🎣
Jennette's Pier ~12 min north
Concrete pier with aquarium-run exhibits — and the source of this page's tide data. Walk-on pass is a couple bucks.
🏜️
Jockey's Ridge State Park ~15 min north
Tallest sand dunes on the East Coast. Go for sunset — kids and dogs love running down the dunes. Free.
🍦
Surfin' Spoon ~12 min north
The OBX frozen yogurt institution, owned by pro surfer Jesse Hines. Expect a line — worth it.
🥓
Sam & Omie's ~12 min north
Serving breakfast to fishermen since 1937. Get there early; the she-crab soup and biscuits are the move.
⛳
Mutiny Bay Adventure Golf ~15 min north
Pirate-themed mini golf with flaming torches at night. Reliable crowd-pleaser for the teen contingent.
